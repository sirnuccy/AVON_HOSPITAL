About Us

Company Background

Avon Medical Practice is a leading multispecialty healthcare provider based in Lagos, Nigeria. We work hard to provide for your health and well-being and to ensure you experience the ultimate care. Our friendly, highly professional staff are committed to maintaining a welcoming ambience, conducive to healing both mind and body. At Avon Medical Practice, we provide the best patient experience with ultramodern facilities ranging from a full-service hospital, multiple on-site clinics across five states in Nigeria and counting, a state-of-the-art dialysis centre, Avon Dialysis Centre, a reputable pharmacy and a laboratory.

Dr Awele V. Elumelu
Chairperson

As the Chairperson of Avon Healthcare Limited, Nigeria’s leading health insurance provider, and the Founder/CEO of Avon Medical Practice, a growing network of full-service clinics and onsite facilities with corporate institutions, she is focused on expanding and improving access to quality healthcare in Nigeria, Africa’s most populous nation.

Dr Ijeoma Dozie
Medical Director

Dr Ijeoma Dozie is a graduate of the University of Jos, Plateau state, from where she obtained her Bachelor’s in Medicine and Surgery. She is a Consultant Family Physician and a fellow of the Postgraduate Medical College of Nigeria. Dr Dozie holds many certifications, both local and internationally; She is an International Board-certified lifestyle medicine physician, a London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE) certified MBA essentials holder, and a United Nations Development Program certified sexuality educator, counsellor, and trainer. In addition, she is a certified Marriage Theologian and Christian counsellor, and a published author in both domestic and international journals including the British Journal of Medicine and Medical Research.

Pharm. Godfrey Imeokparia
Chief Operating Officer

Pharm. Godfrey Imeokparia is the Chief Operating Officer of Avon Medical Practice Limited. In this role, Pharm. Imeokparia is responsible for the management and implementation of the organization’s strategy and directs its overall operations to meet the organization’s mission. He holds the Bachelor of Pharmacy (B. Pharm) degree from the University of Benin and is a Certified Master Procurement Manager (Ch. MPM) and Chartered Master Supply Chain Manager (Ch. SCM).
